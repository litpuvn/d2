Our People

Meet the people behind this project.


Fang Jin, Ph.D
Assistant Professor
Fang Jin received her Ph.D degree from CS@Virginia Tech in 2016. She is fortunate to be advised by Dr. Naren Ramakrishnan in the Discovery Analytics Center. She is broadly interested in data mining and machine Learning. Her research has been focused on information propagation modeling, graph mining, anomaly detection, and spatiotemporal data analysis. Generally speaking, her research seeks to apply data mining into big data to discover deeper insights for social good.

Long Nguyen
PhD Student
Long received his Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology at the President University (Indonesia) in 2005 and then his Master’s degree in Computer Science at Politecnico di Milano, Italy in 2009. He had worked over 10 years in software industry in different countries (such as Singapore, Vietnam, and Italy) before starting to pursue PhD degree in Jan 2017. He has a great interest in data analytics especially regarding to text and social network data.

Zhou Yang
PhD Student
Zhou Yang received his bachelor’s degree in Management (Management Science) at Wuhan University (China) in 2013, and he graduated from University of Florida with a master’s degree in Industrial Engineering in 2015. Before pursuing his Ph.D degree at Texas Tech University in August 2017, he was a big data analyst in China Unicom (China). He has a great interest in machine learning and its application to industries.
